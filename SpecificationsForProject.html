Abstract

An in-class Q&A app that will help professor and students to communicate better.

Background

My persona is about a student and a professor who both want to have something that can encourage students to ask question or answer questions in class. For how to solving that problem, I found out a way which is to make an app that allow students and professors to communicate easier in class.

Wireframe

In order to let students communicate with professor better. I created an app that allow students to post questions online and then transfer the questions to professor. Moreover all students can see the questions other post and "like" the question if they have the same concern. Then professor can select the most popular question among all to answer. And this is how I think the app should look like.

IAmap

There is some specifications about this app than other similar app. It allows students to answers questions posted by other students. Moreover, it allows students to add questions on the slides for others to see it clearly. The slides on the app is highlightable.
